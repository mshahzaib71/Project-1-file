About Al-Hadi Photography



Alhadiphotography.com is a group of highly experienced and certified professional photographers of Karachi. We are the pioneers in candid photojournalism. We believe wedding photography is all about sensing and capturing precious moment of the event for future view of the bride and groom as they are busy in their own rituals. On wedding events bride and groom are totally busy in their own rituals that they are not able to witness all the laughers, emotions and moments of joys from their own eyes. So our wedding photography should be exactly like as if the bride and groom are at their wedding function.
